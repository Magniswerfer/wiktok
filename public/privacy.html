Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Summary

WikiTok runs entirely in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers.

What We Don't Collect

  • No personal information
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No cookies (except for local functionality)
  • No advertising identifiers

Local Storage

WikiTok uses your browser's localStorage to save:

  • Your saved articles
  • App preferences (audio settings, etc.)
  • Cached article data for offline use

This data never leaves your device. You can clear it anytime via your browser settings.

Third-Party Services

WikiTok fetches content directly from:

  • Wikipedia API – Article content and images
  • Pexels API – Background videos (if enabled)

These services have their own privacy policies. Your requests go directly to them from your browser.

Questions? Open an issue on our GitHub repository.
